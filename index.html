We invest selectively — up to $10M per deal, with an indefinite time horizon — in public and private equities.

Our investment thesis is simple. For growth, we invest in strong teams building the future. For value, we invest in attractively priced assets with cash flow and a moat.

We're based in Europe and invest globally. You can contact us at hi@capitalbrothers.org.
